# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Examples of expected behavior:

- provide actionable feedback
- discuss ideas, not people
- respect different experience levels
- acknowledge uncertainty and evidence limits

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- intentionally misleading technical claims

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for violations.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related communication channels.
